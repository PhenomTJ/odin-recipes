Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This chicken noodle soup recipe with bouillon broth is delicious and very easy to make. It is wonderful to prepare for friends or neighbors.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 12 cups water
  • 4 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 9 cubes chicken bouillon
  • ¼ cup chopped carrots
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon dried marjoram
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 8 ounces egg noodles

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large stockpot over medium-high heat. Sauté celery and onion in hot butter until slightly softened. Add water, chicken, bouillon, carrots, parsley, marjoram, pepper, and bay leaf. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Stir in noodles and simmer until pasta is tender, about 10 more minutes.
